KW Lab

We dig data.

At the KW Lab, we focus on building awesome products that bring humanity to banking in Canada. We’re nimble and fresh like a start-up. The difference is, we’re backed by a large company – Capital One®.

What are our projects like?

Everything is about hands-on experimentation with real data and real customers. When our team isn’t dreaming up innovative tech solutions, we’re partnering with other Capital One teams to tackle their biggest challenges.

Our projects are at the intersection of data and digital experiences, so we are looking for someone who sees the potential in how data can be used to help our customers succeed. In the past, we have worked on testing new data products with customers, performing text analytics on customer comments and customizing experiences for cardholders travelling overseas.
