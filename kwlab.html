KW Lab

We dig data.

At the KW Lab, we focus on building awesome products that bring humanity to banking in Canada. We're nimble and fresh like a start-up. The difference is, we're backed by a large company – Capital One®.

What are our projects like?

Everything is about hands-on experimentation with data. When our team isn't dreaming up innovative tech solutions, we're partnering with other Capital One teams to tackle their biggest challenges.

Our projects include, but aren't limited to, performing text analytics on customer comments using machine learning and customizing experiences for cardholders travelling overseas.

Our work is used in the real world – it has a direct impact on our customers.

What's in it for you?

Over the course of four months, you'll work with a small team on one big project. You'll be deeply involved in the work – from ideation to fruition. Now that's ownership!

We use state-of-the-art technologies and have a great office located within the Breithaupt block of downtown Kitchener. Some students love it here so much, that they've decided to come back for a second co-op term. What's more, some have even become full-time Capital One associates!
